Eiffel Tower, Paris

The Eiffel Tower is an iconic symbol of Paris and one of the most famous landmarks in the world. It offers breathtaking views of the city.

Great Wall of China, China

The Great Wall of China is a historic fortification and one of the Seven Wonders of the World. It stretches over 13,000 miles and showcases ancient Chinese engineering.

Statue of Liberty, New York

The Statue of Liberty is an iconic symbol of freedom and democracy, located on Liberty Island in New York Harbor. It was a gift from France to the United States.

Taj Mahal, India

The Taj Mahal is a stunning white marble mausoleum in Agra, India. It was built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.
